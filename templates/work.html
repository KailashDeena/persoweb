001 : DESIGN AND ANALYSIS OF CONDENSER BRAZE FRAME

Hanon Corporation is an American global automotive parts company. The Company mainly specializes in manufacturing Automobile parts like AC units and Instrument Panels, Door trims. It supplies parts to various organizations including HYUNDAI, FORD and NISSAN. At the Chennai plant the company manufactures the dashboards of Hyundai i10, i20, VERNA, Nissan MICRA, SUNNY and Renault DUSTER. The Ford FIGO receives its Front and Rear door trims from this firm.

The objective of our project is based on braze frame which is an important part of condenser manufacturing process, as the condenser is produced by the brazing process. So, we have planned to analysis the existing frame and checking out their design parameters to develop the existing frame by changing the frame material and comparing them to attain the better result.
