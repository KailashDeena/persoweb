002 : DESIGN AND FABRICATION OF HYBRID SAIL

The wind and solar energy are omnipresent, freely available, and environmental friendly. The combined utilization of these renewable energy sources are therefore becoming increasingly attractive and are being widely used as alternative of oil-produced energy. A renewable hybrid sail consists of two energy sources, a controller and an optional energy storage system. These hybrid sails are becoming popular due to advancements in renewable energy technologies and substantial rise in prices of petroleum products.

The aim of this project is to develop a eco-friendly ship that uses renewable energy. The ship also consists of automatic mast mechanism to sail according to the wind direction. This Project also highlights the future developments, which have the potential to increase the economic attractiveness of such eco-friendly ships and their acceptance by marine industries.
